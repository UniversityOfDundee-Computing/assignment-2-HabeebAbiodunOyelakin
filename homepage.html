Chicken Pepperoni

Chicken pepperoni is a type of pepperoni made from chicken meat. It's a variation of the traditional pork-based pepperoni but is made with ground chicken and seasoned with spices such as paprika, chili powder, fennel, and garlic, among others.

It tends to be lower in fat than traditional pork pepperoni and is a popular alternative for people looking for a leaner option or those who do not consume pork due to dietary restrictions or personal preferences. Chicken pepperoni can be used as a topping on pizzas, added to sandwiches, salads, or enjoyed as a snack on its own.
